What it is: How to identify user traits from their answers.

What it contains:

Inference Process: Step-by-step method to analyze responses

Pattern Collection: Gather themes across all life stages

Cross-Life-Stage Validation: Check if a trait shows up in multiple stages

Confidence Assessment: How sure are we about each trait?

Output Format: How to present trait findings

Example: If user as a child always asked "why" and as a teenager got lost in research and as an adult loves deep dives → "Consistent intellectual curiosity pattern" (High confidence)

Why you need it: This is how you go from stories to insights. It's your analytical engine.
