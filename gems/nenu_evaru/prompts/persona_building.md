What it is: How to synthesize traits into a coherent persona.

What it contains:

Persona Components: Core identity, operating style, potential, tensions

Persona Crafting Prompts: How to write a meaningful persona

Output Template: The exact structure for the persona profile

Validation Questions: How to check if the persona resonates

Example:
Core Identity: "The Curious Explorer"
What Drives You: Understanding how things work, connecting ideas, sharing knowledge
Operating Style: Deep dives, collaborative exploration, thoughtful questioning
Why you need it: Traits alone are data. A persona is understanding. This makes the insights feel human.
